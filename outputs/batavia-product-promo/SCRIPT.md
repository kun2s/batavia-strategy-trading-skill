## Optional Voiceover

Trading agents are getting faster, but their reasoning is still too hard to audit.

Batavia turns CoinMarketCap evidence into a deterministic strategy receipt.

It records the asset, the tools used, freshness, regime, sizing, invalidation rules, rejected alternatives, and an evidence hash.

Fresh confirmed uptrend? Active. Extreme fear? Stand aside. Stale data? Insufficient data.

No wallet. No signing. No forced confidence.

Batavia is the control layer before a trading bot: CoinMarketCap evidence in, auditable strategy receipt out.
